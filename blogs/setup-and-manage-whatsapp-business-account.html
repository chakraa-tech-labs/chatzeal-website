Introduction to WhatsApp Business
WhatsApp has revolutionized the way people connect and businesses are no exception. With nearly 80% of adults now preferring messaging to interact with a business, WhatsApp Business offers a powerful opportunity to connect with your customers and stay ahead in an increasingly competitive market.
In today’s fast paced customer driven digital era, effective communication is crucial to any business. WhatsApp Business is one such platform for businesses to interact with customers in real-time offering personalized and secured communication. Whether you are a small or mid-sized business owner new to WhatsApp Business or looking to optimize your existing account, this comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about setting up and managing your WhatsApp Business account effectively. Let’s dive in to know how WhatsApp Business can level up your communication strategy.
WhatsApp currently offers two business solutions: WhatsApp Business App (for small groups) and WhatsApp Business API (for medium and large enterprises that need automation and integrations).
What is WhatsApp Business?
WhatsApp Business app is designed for small and mid-sized businesses to facilitate direct and seamless communication with their customers. Along with the core functionalities of the standard WhatsApp messenger, WhatsApp Business further offers business-oriented tools tailored for business to help them manage and engage with their customers efficiently.WhatsApp Business App is ideal for small businesses as it only accommodates 5 devices per account. Large businesses that handle large contact volumes usually utilize WhatsApp Business API for automation and scalability.
What is WhatsApp Business API?
WhatsApp Business API is a robust, scalable solution designed to meet the needs of large businesses that need advanced communication functionalities. This offers a fast, secure and reliable platform to interact with customers all over the world. This API enables businesses to seamlessly integrate WhatsApp with their Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system enabling them to send automated messages at scale, run advanced campaigns and streamlining customer support operations.
WhatsApp Business API is built on the same infrastructure as the WhatsApp Messenger. This is one of the major advantages of WhatsApp Business API for its ability to handle high volumes of customer interactions, making it ideal for businesses with a large client base. It enables customer service teams to send and receive messages efficiently while even managing all customer interactions directly through WhatsApp. However this API needs an official partnership with WhatsApp and is often used by large businesses that need to handle voluminous customer interactions.

Setting up your WhatsApp Business account
The first step to start exploring WhatsApp Business is to set up an account. Getting started to create a Business account in WhatsApp Business app is a straightforward process, the following are the steps to create one:
1.Download the app
Download the WhatsApp Business app from Google Play store or Apple App store and launch it.
2.Verify your phone number
Enter your business phone number that is not already used for any other WhatsApp account.
3.Set up your business profile
Fill in your business information, such as your business name, profile picture, category, description and business address.
Additional information about your business
Optionally you can further optimize your business profile in the business tools section by adding in additional information like your business hours and website. Including this information will help your customers to know the best times to reach out to you and provide quick access to your website where they can learn more about your offerings.
Setting up WhatsApp Business API
1.Choose a business solution provider
You need an authorized WhatsApp Business Solution provider like ChatZeal, Sleekflow, WATI or Sinch to help you access and implement the API.
2.Verify your business
You need to verify your business via Facebook Business. Submit the relevant documents to confirm your business identity.
Business verification
Businesses using the API can apply for green tick verification to enhance their business account credibility. Apply for a verified badge from WhatsApp to help your customers to know that you are a legitimate business.

Ensuring Data Privacy and Security
  • Understanding WhatsApp Business data privacy policies
    WhatsApp utilizes end to end encryption which ensures the conversations between you and your customers remain secure and private. This means that the message is encrypted from the sender’s device and is decrypted only by the recipient’s device with no intermediary access to the message content including WhatsApp even if it passes the WhatsApp’s servers.
  • Securing customer data and conversations
    It is important to use WhatsApp’s end to end encryption to protect customer data. Implement strong security practices and restrict access to the customer data by allowing only authorized team members to handle the data.
  • Implementing best practices for data security
    Avoid sharing sensitive customer data over chat unless necessary. Follow data security practices by regularly monitoring the log access and following the two factor authentication. Ensure that the third party integrations meet the security standards. By adopting these practices and training your employees on data security, you can safeguard your customer data and build trust in your business.
